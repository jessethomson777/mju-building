06 / Process
Our simple process

Five steps from first call to final clean-up.

We keep it dead simple. No mystery, no surprise invoices, no “while we’re here” add-ons you didn’t ask for.

01

Free Site Inspection & Quote

We come to you, measure up, talk through options, and send a detailed written quote within 48 hours.

02

Site Prep & Excavation

Cut, fill, level. The foundation work that decides how your concrete will perform for the next 30 years.

03

Formwork & Reinforcement

Set-out, formwork, mesh and bar to engineered spec. Falls double-checked before we book the truck.

04

Pour & Finish

Right mix, right slump, right finish — plain, honed, exposed aggregate or coloured.

05

Final Clean Up

Forms stripped, site swept, off-cuts removed. Handover with a smile and a cure schedule.

07 / Finishes
Concrete finishes

Concrete finishes to suit every project.

Pick a finish to match the architecture and the budget. We’ll walk you through samples on site before you commit.

Plain

Hard-trowel grey. Strong, simple, and most cost-effective. Ideal for shed slabs and back-of-house work.

Exposed Aggregate

Textured, slip-resistant, premium look. The popular pick for driveways and pool surrounds.

Coloured

Through-coloured oxides in earth tones. Pairs beautifully with brick, stone and timber.

Honed

Polished, smooth, modern. Showcases the aggregate beneath. Built for entertaining areas.
